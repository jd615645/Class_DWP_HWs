Overview

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering at National Chung-Hsing University was founded in July, 1992. The department currently has eighteen faculty members, offering MS program, Ph. D program and undergraduate program of Computer Science. The department aims at

  • pioneering basic research of computer science & engineering and becoming a major academic research center in the computer area.
  • working closely with computer, electronic and mechanics industry for designing innovative products and promoting manufacturing technology of R.O.C.
